Short Biography

I am a composer and musical performer, currently based in Milan, Italy.

I am pursuing a PhD in Composition and Performance at Pescara Conservatory. My research project investigates theoretical and conceptual frameworks on New Media from a sociological perspective.

Education

  • Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado, Milano - BA and MA in Composition
  • Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado, Milano - Cello and Conducting
  • Composition/Cello/Conducting Masterclasses and Seminars (available on full CV)
  • Conservatorio L. D'Annunzio, Pescara - Doctorate in Composition and Performance (candidate 2025-2028)

Selection of recent works

  • 2025 - "Pomerio" - Voice and mixed media
  • 2023 - "Thrustebrai" - Amplified ensemble
  • 2022 - "With Fleshy Belts And Reins" - Voice, Dancers and mixed media
